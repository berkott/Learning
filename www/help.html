What you can do with our app
With our app you can reserve books, checkoutbooks, see a map of the school library, and you will see reminders on when your books are overdue.
How to reserve books
In order to reserve book, all you must do is simply search for the book you would like to have in the search bar on the home page, and then just hit the reserve button. You will then have reserved the book for 1 month.
How to checkout a book
If you wish to checkout a book, you must have the physical copy that you would like to check out. Once you have the physical copy, all you have to do is scan the barcode and follow the instructions that follow. You will have checked out your book for one month.
How to see the map
In order to see the school library map, simply navigate to the map page using the navigation at the bottom.
How to see reminders
You can see a variety of reminders on the home page.
What the administrator can do
The administrator will be the one who can change the books that are available, manage accounts, and change the school map. In order to do this, all the administrator must do is log into Firebase and from there they can make all those changes and more. It is also very easy to use because it has a simple and intuitive user interface.
